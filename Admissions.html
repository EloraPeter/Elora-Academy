Admissions

Join us and unlock your potential in the world of technology

How to Enroll

Visit our admissions office or apply online. Requirements include academic transcripts and a passion for tech.

Application Process

Our application process is designed to be straightforward and accessible. Follow these steps to complete your application:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents such as academic transcripts and recommendation letters.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Schedule an interview with our admissions team.

Important Dates

Keep track of these important dates to ensure you don't miss any deadlines:

  • Application Deadline: June 1st
  • Interview Dates: June 10th - June 20th
  • Notification of Acceptance: July 1st
  • Orientation Week: August 1st - August 7th

Scholarships and Financial Aid

We offer a variety of scholarships and financial aid options to support our students. Visit our financial aid office or apply online to see what you qualify for.

  • Merit-based Scholarships: Awarded based on academic excellence.
  • Need-based Financial Aid: Available for students who demonstrate financial need.
  • Special Scholarships: For students excelling in specific fields such as STEM or Arts.
